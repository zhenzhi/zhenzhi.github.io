Express Pre-Checkout

 

Problem To Solve

Imagine a time you are trying to buy something on a new website, as a new user, you have to create a new account, and enter contact and payment info to checkout. This can be tedious for you. We think PayPal can help a lot of consumers like you to express the checkout and simplify the first-time purchasing experience.

Project Goals

Increase the PayPal selection rate among other payment methods

My Role

I am the design lead on this project. I am responsible for all the design directions and collaboration with product managers, researchers, content designers, engineers, and partner teams. I also mentored and collaborated with a junior designer on this project.

HMW

PayPal is a payment network across consumers and merchants. Our solutions are not only for consumers but also for merchants.
How do we provide first-time merchant consumers a low-friction purchase experience throughout the journey and help merchants to have a better conversion?
