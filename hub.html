Summary

Voyager Hub is a new product to manage the enterprise search features.

Context

Since the initial Voyager product was launched our customers and prospective customers have experienced massive growth in the amount structured and unstructured data they need to index with a search and discovery platform such as Voyager. A better approach to manage the process of building a single large index without the administrator having to configure federation or to run merge tasks after multiple indexes have been created.That's the reason we want craete a new product called voyager hub.
