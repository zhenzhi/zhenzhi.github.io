Summary

Notegether is a tablet application for people easily to take notes in the class. You can record audio and video simultaneously when you taking the notes. It can also auto-check the completion of the task and send notifications through calendar. Notegether, we handle all the notes!

Problems to solve

Note-takers in Round Rock independent school district still use the traditional pen and paper taking notes. The biggest problem is digitalizing all the notes together. Notegether is designed to solve the problem of inconsistency and incompletion of notes.

How can I handle seperate images and texts?

Taking notes, inserting images in the right place. Making images connected to texts.

How can I record and take notes simultaneously?

Recording audio and video when you taking notes. You don't need two apps.

What if I forget some tasks?

Auto check completion. Don't be afraid missing anything.

How do I manage my projects?

Sending notifications, scheduling with calendar, locating with map. We manage all the project.
