Summary

Notegether is a tablet application for people easily to make notes. You can record audio and video simultaneously when you taking the notes. It can also auto-check the completion of the task and send notifications through calendar. Notegether, taking notes can not be easier!

Problems to solve

Note-takers in Round Rock independent school district still use the traditional pen and paper taking notes. The biggest problem is digitalizing all the notes together. Notegether is designed to solve the problem of inconsistency and incompletion of notes.
