Accelerated
One-Click Checkout

 

My Role

As the design lead for this project, I oversaw the entire design process, from concept to implementation. I guided a small team of designers, including one mid-level designer, one junior designer, and one content designer, as well as a dedicated researcher. Additionally, I collaborated closely with four product managers and twelve engineers to ensure a seamless integration of design and development.

Problem to Solve

70% of users drop-off at Checkout.
Consumers want a fast, easy, and consistent checkout solution across merchants.
So, merchants are exploring solutions from providers like Bolt, Shopify and Stripe that optimize their checkout funnel.


Competitor Landscape

One-Click checkout is an e-commerce feature that allows customers to complete a purchase with just one click. This is achieved by storing the customer's payment and shipping information securely so that it doesn't need to be entered each time they make a purchase.
One-Click checkout is not a new concept, and there are several established players in the market like Bolt, Shoppay and Link by Stripe.


Opportunity and Strategy

Accelerated checkout will transform the checkout experience for both consumers and merchants. By leveraging the power of our network effect, which encompasses over 432M active consumer accounts and 35M active merchants, we can create a flywheel that drives further adoption and enhances the checkout experience for all users.


The Goal

Create a friction-free checkout that reduces drop-offs & is easy to integrate across merchants

Design Principle



Meet Connect By PayPal

Quick Sign Up for PayPal Users
Quick Sign Up for Guest(Non-PayPal) Users
One-Click Checkout for Return(Connect) Users



Connect by PayPal lets you quickly sign up and pay during checkout. It can automatically fill in your checkout details with a one-time password (OTP) and lets you easily switch to a different address or payment card if needed.

The Results

Connect by PayPal made its debut on the BigCommerce platform in Q3 2023. Early data from the first month reveals an impressive 16% average uplift in conversion rates across various merchants. Moreover, Connect users have experienced a remarkable 40% reduction in checkout time compared to those without Connect.
