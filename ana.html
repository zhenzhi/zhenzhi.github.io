Enterprise Data Visualization

Summary

Voyager Search is a leading global provider of geospatial, enterprise search solutions that enable a single point of search across all of your content repositories. With Voyager, users can search, find, and deliver more than 2,000 types of content. Built on top of Solr/Lucene, the world’s premier open source solution, Voyager offers Fortune 500 businesses, government entities, and multinational organizations easy-to-use search, data management, document delivery, and map visualization capabilities – including Natural Language Processing. Voyager Analytics is a new extension which allows users to visualize your data in a variety of ways, such as the distribution of data types by location or the distribution of indexing errors over time. Apart from the distribution of data attributes, Analytics can also show how users have interacted with your data, including viewing, downloading or liking/disliking.

 

As the only designer in the team, I led the design from the initial idea to the prototyping. I collaborated with product owner and developers to define the product need and push the pixels.

Context

Voyager is a powerful search engine that can index billions of records, but with volume, comes confusion. Executive management will want to know how much they have of each thing as well as who used it, when and how. They want to know how content collections have changed over time and how they were distributed geographically. Ultimately, we will also want to use user behavior metrics to influence relevancy of the search results selected.
