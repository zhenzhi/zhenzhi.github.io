Introduction

Over 6 years old, AT&T's design library required a redesign. My team was working on the next generation of user identity project and we want users to experience the modern and fresh new design style. At that time, some of the design components are not ready yet, so we have to create a new component from scratch and contribute that to the AT&T design library.

Design Principles


Relevant: Anticipate and account for our customer's needs and behaviors. 
Purposeful: Create with just enough and when in doubt, remove. 
Human: Build authentic, relevant experiences that connect people in familiar and friendly ways. 
Inclusive: Create the best experience for the greatest number of people.
Premium: Simplify the experience to focus, guide, and showcase.
Consistent: Use standard, common elements that are familiar and align with people's expectations.
