Senior Mobility
User Research


Summary

This project has applied design thinking methodology to generate solultions. Our focus is senior mobility needs and issues they have. A ride sharing concept designed exclusively for seniors can better suit the unique set of needs that seniors have when dealing with mobility. These may involve trips to appointments on a more frequent basis, regular shopping errands, visiting friends or family, attending community functions, and so on. These solutions may also provide a method of aiding a person’s quality of life and give back the intangible sense of independence that many seniors are lacking without efficient mobility.

Problem Space

Seniors face a multitude of mobility issues and are often an overlooked population in regards to technological development and innovations. These problems come in a variety of forms or factors, such as an inability or safety issue with driving their own car, disabilities that impact one’s ability to walk, had recently been in an accident themselves, are too far away from necessary stores to walk yet too close for valid public transportation, and so on. These issues all share mobility as a centralized theme.

Process

Design Research

Innovation Intent

To facilitate seniors’ greater quality of life by removing the feeling of dependency or imposition on others and preventing isolation with the use of a ridesharing/e-hail service. To provide a mobility service that caters to the needs of senior populations. This will also assist children or caretakers to ensure peace of mind with concerns to mobility.
