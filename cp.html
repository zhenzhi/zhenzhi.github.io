New Deposit Flow

The Project Goal

The deposit experience is essential to the BlockFi platform. Like all the bank/investment account, users need to deposit some amounts of money to BlockFi then get the values like earn interest from it. The goal of the project is to reduce the friction of deposit flow to make sure users can fund accounts easily.

The Before

Based on some data I get from user testing, customer service and social media, I identified four major issues that added the friction on the deposit flow.

 

 

1. The order of the steps slow down the workflow
Over 70% of deposit activities are going through a bank account(ACH) to deposit US dollars. Asking users to select all the currencies as the first steps would be inefficient for most of the deposit activities. In the future, BlockFi will support more and more different cryptos, so the dropdown will be longer and longer. Giving users a lot of unrelated choices won't make the deposit flow easier.

 

 

2. Redundant steps make the workflow cumbersome
In the previous design, we asked users which bank account they want to deposit from every time they make a deposit. This is not necessarily based on the data that users won't change the bank they use most of the time.

 

 

3. Repeated and lengthy copy make the communication unclear
We showed the same transaction details and withdrawal notes on both of the confirmation and success steps repeatedly which is not very helpful. Some users mentioned this content looks very wordy and hard to have a focus.

 

 

4. Inconsistent and unappealing visual design
In the previous design, a lot of the pages didn't have a very good visual hierarchy and consistent elements which will make the experience untrustworthy.

 

The After




Issue: The order of the steps slow down the workflow
Solution: New organized workflow for the deposit, fewer clicks for the majority of activities







Issue: Redundant steps make the workflow cumbersome
Solution: Pre-select options and cut unnecessary steps to speed up the flow







Issue: Repeated and lengthy copy make the communication unclear
Solution: Remove repeated information and communicate in a more concise and clear way







Issue: Inconsistent and unappealing visual design
Solution: Apply new design system on all the designs





The Results

The new design got very good feedback from testing compared to the previous design. Most of the users mentioned that the design was simple and straightforward. We targeted to release this new design in the middle of Q3. At that time, we will have more quantitative data on task success rate and time on task to compare with the current one.

There are also some other opportunities to improve the deposit flow like adding other payments like PayPal, debit card, and some other international payments, but this involves more backend work in a short time. So we hold that idea for now and plan it for the future.

Fund account on onboarding

The Project Goal

Less than 30% of users fund their account after created their accounts in 1 week. From a product strategy perspective, we wanted users to fund their accounts as early as possible so they can start getting the value from BlockFi. We think onboarding flow could have some opportunities to encourage users to fund their accounts.
The goal of this project is to get more first funds from users in the first week.

The Research

Based on data I get from the user survey and customer supports, here are some of the reasons users don't fund their accounts in the first week.

1. Not motivated and didn't see the value to fund account earlier
2. Forget to fund the account after some time
3. Need time to trust platform
4. Money was not ready at the time when created an account

The Design




Show the value, encourage users to fund their account during onboarding







Checklist as a reminder after users sign in to the accounts

The Results

From the user testing, most of the users mentioned that it was helpful to ask them to fund accounts and also show them a checklist later on. They also mentioned the high interest was something that got their attention. We released this feature in March 2021, the fund account rate has increased 44.9% in the first month of release.
